Hello, I’m Ayaan!

I’m an aspiring data scientist and economist with a strong focus on international trade, macroeconomics, and public policy. TheMetrician is a passion project of mine where I seek to apply both traditional and cutting-edge econometric and data science methods to address contemporary economic and political challenges. It serves as a platform that informs policymakers of the analytical tools at their disposal and how they can be applied in different contexts. A core theme of TheMetrician is finding compelling stories in data. To achieve this, the platform focuses on providing intelligible insights through appealing, self- explanatory data visuals. I hope this platform serves as a useful resource for those at the junction of data science and economics.
